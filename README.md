# Bike Sales Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing bike sales by category, product, and year.  
It helps identify top-selling products, track sales trends, and support business decisions.

## Overview

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| *Purpose* | Analyze bike sales performance visually |
| *Tools* | Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel/CSV |
| *Focus* | Categories, products, sales trends, yearly comparison |

## Dashboard Features

- KPI cards for categories, sub-categories, and products
- Donut chart for sales by category
- Bar chart for sales by product
- Line chart for monthly sales trends
- Product details table
- Interactive slicers for category, sub-category, product, and year

## Key Insights

- Bikes have the highest sales compared to other categories.
- Mountain-200 and Touring-1000 models show strong performance.
- Monthly trends help track changes in customer demand.
- Year filters allow comparison from 2011 to 2014.

## Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses improve reporting, identify best-selling products, compare categories, and support inventory and marketing decisions.
